See everything that moves money in your account—card spend, payments out, deposits, withdrawals, and more—in one place.

## What you can do

**List and filter** — Pull transaction history with filters (customer, date, type, status). Paginate through results or export to CSV.

**Check status** — Each transaction has a status: success, processing, or failed.

**Get receipts** — Generate a receipt for any transaction to share or keep for records.

## What shows up

Transactions include card activity (purchases, deposits, withdrawals), bank and other outbound payments, virtual account deposits, payment link and invoice payments, and similar activity. Each row has an ID, reference, amount, currency, type, status, timestamp, and optional customer or description details.
